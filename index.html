About KDJ Salon

We're more than just a salon - we're a destination for transformation, relaxation, and self-care.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by master stylist Kamal De Silva, KDJ Salon has grown from a small boutique to one of the most sought-after beauty destinations in the region. Our commitment to excellence and personalized service has earned us a loyal clientele.

We believe that beauty should be accessible to everyone, which is why we offer a range of services at various price points without compromising on quality.
